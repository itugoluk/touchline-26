# Touchline '26

A World Cup 2026 strategy game. Take charge of any of the 48 qualified nations and manage them through the real tournament format — twelve groups, a 32-team knockout, and a final at MetLife Stadium on July 19. Or pick nobody and just watch the summer unfold.

## The game

- **Real 2026 draw.** All twelve groups match the final draw held in Washington D.C. on December 5, 2025, from Mexico–South Africa–South Korea–Czechia in Group A to England–Croatia–Ghana–Panama in Group L. Squads feature each nation's actual key players.
- **Tactical control.** Formation (4-3-3, 4-2-3-1, 4-4-2, 3-5-2, 5-4-1), mentality, pressing line, playing style, attack focus, and your set-piece taker. Every choice has a real trade-off in the simulation: high pressing tires after the 70th minute, counter-attacking punishes teams that overcommit, building through your talisman lifts you against weaker sides but gets you marked out against stronger ones.
- **Live matches.** Minute-by-minute simulation with a live feed, momentum bar, and bookmaker odds computed from the same model the match runs on. Adjust everything at half-time and around the 70th minute, and spend two one-shot bench moves — fresh legs to lift your attack, or a defensive change to blunt theirs. The opposition manager adjusts too.
- **Board expectations.** Every squad carries a target based on its strength, from semi-finals for the top seeds to simply competing for the minnows. At the end of your run, the board delivers its verdict.
- **The whole tournament, not just your corner.** Results from around the groups after every matchday, all twelve tables, a live Golden Boot race, and a full tournament review — complete bracket, final tables, and top scorers — whether you lift the trophy or fly home early.
- **Spectator mode.** No dugout, no pressure: step through the tournament round by round, or fast-forward straight to the final whistle in New Jersey.
- **Full tournament structure.** Top two from each group advance plus the eight best third-placed sides. Knockouts run through extra time and penalty shootouts, with your chosen taker stepping up first.
